Firehouse 2010-11-24

Facebook event page

I was inspired to start DJing again after Charles’ Facebook post about the quality of the music at the Firehouse. For this set, I focused on representing classic swing music, and on making sure that every single song was a prime example of top-quality musicianship.

  1. Benny Goodman – Jam Session
  2. Jimmie Lunceford – Taint Watcha Do
  3. Lincoln Center Jazz Orchestra – C Jam Blues
  4. Kid Ory – Wang Wang Blues
  5. Charlie Barnet – Oh Miss Jaxson
  6. Jimmie Lunceford – Lunceford Special
  7. Jimmie Lunceford – Muddy Water
  8. Earl “Fatha” Hines – Squeeze Me (edit)
  9. Bunny Berigan – (How To Make Love In) Ten Easy Lessons
  10. Woody Herman – Woodchopper’s Ball
  11. Count Basie – Splanky
  12. Tommy Dorsey – Minor Goes Muggin
  13. Louis Jordan – Stone Cold Dead in the Market
  14. John Kirby – Effervescent Blues
  15. New Orleans Jazz Vipers – Blue Drag
  16. Tommy Dorsey – Stomp it Off
  17. Ramsey Lewis – Wade In The Water
  18. Count Basie – Sweets
  19. Kid Ory – Girls Go Crazy
