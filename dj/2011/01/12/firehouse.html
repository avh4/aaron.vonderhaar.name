Firehouse 2011-01-12

Facebook event page

Recently inspired by the Dixieland Jazz Festival (and having bought about 20 CDs there and shortly after), I wanted to do a set focusing on the excellent swing bands that are playing today. I also wanted to push the boundaries of “20% other” more than I had in my previous set.

  1. Mora’s Modern Rhythmists – Cavernism
  2. Jonathan Stout – Royal Family
  3. Washboard Sam – I’ve Been Treated Wrong
  4. Dinah Washington – I’ll Never Stop Loving You
  5. Mora’s Modern Rhythmists – The Dipsey Doodle
  6. Jimmie Lunceford – Oh Boy
  7. Jonathan Stout – Moppin’ and Boppin’
  8. Carl Sonny Leyland – Hot Muffins
  9. Uptown Lowdown Jazz Band – Radio
  10. Reynolds Brothers – I Would Do Anything For You
  11. Benny Goodman – Jam Session
  12. Ben l’Oncle Soul – Seven Nation Army
  13. Melody Gardot – Who Will Comfort Me
  14. Jonathan Stout – I’ll See You In My Dreams
  15. Reynolds Brothers – Douce Ambiance
  16. Washboard Sam – Rockin’ My Blues Away
  17. Sidney Bechet – Blues My Naughty Sweetie Gives to Me
  18. Michael Franti & Spearhead – Say Hey
