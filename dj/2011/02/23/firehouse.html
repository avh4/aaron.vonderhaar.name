Firehouse 2011-02-23

Facebook event page

I wanted this set to feature Louis Jordan and Cab Calloway, since I had avoided those artists in my recent sets at the Firehouse. I wanted to start off with moderate tempos as a transition from the beginner-focused set before me, but also push to some really fast songs in the middle. I would have tried for another big tempo push at the end, but instead decided to slow it down to leave people with some energy for Meeshi’s set since he was planning to feature Sidney Bechet.

  1. Jonathan Stout – Moppin’ and Boppin’
  2. Louis Jordan – Cole Slaw
  3. Jimmie Lunceford – Le Jazz Hot
  4. Eddie Condon – Cherry
  5. Tommy Dorsey – Well, Git It!
  6. Jonathan Stout – Hop Skip and Jump
  7. Louis Jordan & Ella Fitzgerald – Stone Cold Dead in the Market (He Had It Coming)
  8. Lincoln Center Jazz Orchestra – C Jam Blues
  9. Sam Cooke – Frankie And Johnny
  10. Jimmie Lunceford – For Dancers Only
  11. Woody Herman – Woodchopper’s Ball
  12. Reynolds Brothers – Dinah
  13. Mora’s Modern Rhythmists – A Viper’s Moan Snowball jam
  14. Michael Bublé – Lazy River
  15. Django Reinhardt – Stardust
  16. Michael Bublé – Spiderman Theme
  17. Louis Jordan – Hungry Man
  18. Lou Rawls – Drink Muddy Water
  19. Nina Simone – Revolution
